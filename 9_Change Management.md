## 9. Change Management
 
### Stakeholder Impact Assessment
 
| Stakeholder | Current Behavior | Required Change | Resistance Level |
|-------------|-----------------|----------------|-----------------|
| Warehouse Staff | Paper-based picking | Barcode scan at every pick | High |
| POS Staff | Manual invoice adjustment | Trust system output | Medium |
| Branch Managers | Not measuring inventory KPIs | Daily variance review | Medium |
| Procurement | Gut-based ordering | Data-driven reorder points | Low |
| IT / ERP Team | Reactive maintenance | Active integration ownership | Medium |
 
### Change Plan (4 Phases)
 
**Phase 1 — Awareness (Week 1–2)**  
Management communication on why this is changing. No training yet — awareness and buy-in only.
 
**Phase 2 — Training (Week 3–4, pilot branch)**  
Hands-on training per role. Test scenarios run before go-live.
 
**Phase 3 — Pilot (Month 2)**  
Single wholesale branch goes live. Daily KPI monitoring. Weekly retrospective with staff.
 
**Phase 4 — Full Rollout (Month 3–6)**  
Branch-by-branch rollout. Supervisors act as internal champions. KPI dashboard visible to all managers.
