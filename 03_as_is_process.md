## 🔄 AS-IS Process (Current State)

### Sales Fulfillment Workflow — Wholesale Branch

[POS Staff] → Creates Draft Order (paper/system)
↓
[Customer] → Takes paper draft → Walks to Warehouse
↓
[Warehouse Staff] → Manually picks items
→ Crosses out unavailable items (❌ manual)
↓
[Customer] → Returns to POS
↓
[POS Staff] → Issues invoice based on available items only

**What this means in practice:**

* No real-time inventory visibility at POS
* Customer physically moves between 2 locations per transaction
* Warehouse staff has no system confirmation — works from paper
* Invoice is generated AFTER physical picking, not before
* Inventory system updated with delays (if at all)
