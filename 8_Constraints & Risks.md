## 8. Constraints & Risks
 
### Implementation Constraints
 
| Constraint | Description | Mitigation |
|-----------|-------------|-----------|
| Custom ERP limitations | In-house ERP may require development for real-time integration | Assess API capability in Week 1; scope development early |
| Barcode infrastructure | No scanners deployed — requires hardware procurement | Budget and procure in parallel with process design |
| Staff resistance | Warehouse staff accustomed to paper | Structured change management (see Section 9) |
| Multi-branch complexity | 7 locations with varying workflows | Pilot in 1 branch before full rollout |
| SKU normalization effort | 10,000+ SKUs require audit and reclassification | Dedicated resource for 3–6 weeks |
 
### Risk Register
 
| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|--------|-----------|
| ERP customization exceeds budget | Medium | High | Cap scope — phase development; config before custom build |
| Staff bypass new system | High | High | Management mandate + training + daily KPI monitoring |
| Data errors during SKU normalization | Medium | High | Dual-run old and new system for 4 weeks post-cutover |
| Pilot results don't generalize | Low | Medium | Document all variance; adjust before full rollout |
| Business disruption at go-live | Medium | High | Go-live on low-volume day; full rollback plan defined |
