## 12. KPI Framework & Expected Results
 
### Primary KPIs
 
| KPI | Baseline (AS-IS) | Target (TO-BE) | Improvement | Measurement Method |
|-----|-----------------|---------------|------------|-------------------|
| Inventory Variance Rate | ~15% avg | < 3% | ~80% reduction | System vs. physical (weekly cycle count) |
| Transaction Completion Time | 15–20 min | 5–8 min | ~60% faster | ERP timestamp: order open → invoice |
| Barcode Scan Compliance | 0% | > 95% | New capability | ERP dispatch log |
| Stockout Incidents / Month | ~30+ | < 10 | ~65% reduction | ERP unfulfilled order log |
| Manual Data Entry Rate | 100% | < 20% | ~80% automation | ERP vs. manual entry count |
| Purchase Order Accuracy | Estimate-based | Data-driven | Significant | Actual received vs. PO quantity |
 
### Secondary KPIs
 
| KPI | Baseline | Target |
|-----|----------|--------|
| Overstock value (SAR) | Uncontrolled | Reduced 30%+ via reorder points |
| Year-end inventory adjustment | High | < 1% of total stock value |
| SKU duplicate rate | High | 0 active duplicates |
| ERP data entry delay | End of day+ | Real-time (< 1 min) |
