## 15. About This Project
 
This case study documents a real operational problem observed across a multi-branch retail and wholesale environment in Saudi Arabia. The problems analyzed — inventory variance, disconnected systems, manual workarounds, and absence of accountability — were not theoretical. They were the daily operational reality across 7 locations handling 10,000+ active SKUs.
 
**What makes this analysis real:**
- The AS-IS process reflects exactly how transactions were handled — including the customer physically walking between POS and warehouse with a paper draft
- Root cause analysis identifies three distinct failure layers: system, process, and governance
- The gap table assigns owners by specific role, not generic department
- Failure scenarios were designed from operational exceptions that occurred regularly
- The implementation roadmap is phased to minimize business disruption during transition
 
**Methodology note:** Figures represent operational estimates derived from direct observation and comparable industry benchmarks. Exact metrics would be baseline-measured through formal system audit at the start of a structured engagement.
