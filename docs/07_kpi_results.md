## 📈 KPI & Expected Results

| KPI                                       | Before                    | After (Target)                | Improvement             |
| ----------------------------------------- | ------------------------- | ----------------------------- | ----------------------- |
| Inventory Variance                        | ~15% avg                  | <3%                           | ~80% reduction          |
| Manual Data Entry Steps                   | 100% manual               | <20% manual                   | ~80% automation         |
| Transaction Completion Time               | ~15–20 min                | ~5–8 min                      | ~60% faster             |
| Stockout Incidents / Month                | Frequent (est. 30+/month) | <10/month                     | ~65% reduction          |
| Overstock Cost                            | High (uncontrolled)       | Controlled via reorder points | Significant reduction   |
| Customer Return Rate (process complaints) | High                      | Low                           | Improved NPS            |
| Purchasing Decision Accuracy              | Based on estimates        | Based on live data            | Reliable demand signals |
