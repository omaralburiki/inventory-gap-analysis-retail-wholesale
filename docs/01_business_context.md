## 🧭 Business Context

A mid-sized trading company operating in the food, beverages, and consumer
goods sector across 7 locations (3 wholesale branches, 2 supermarkets,
2 mini-markets).

**Key Complexity:**

* 10,000+ active SKUs
* Single products with multiple SKUs due to multi-country sourcing
  (same item imported from different origins = different barcodes/codes)
* High transaction volume across all branches
* Mix of wholesale and retail operations in parallel

**System in Use:** Custom-built ERP (legacy, limited automation)
